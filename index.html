About Us

PG ACDADEMY IN COMMERCE is an educational institution which has been operating successfully since 2015 with an emphasis on Commerce subjects in the heart of the city of Thrissur (Round North). Overwhelming teacher-parent meetings, seminars and discussions set us apart. In addition, the institute runs a number of cultural programs for the personal development of the students.
